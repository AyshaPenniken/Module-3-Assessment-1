Garlic Herb Breadsticks

Prep time: 10 mins

Yield: 4

Ingredients





Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a rectangle and place on the baking sheet.
  3. Brush melted butter over the dough, then sprinkle with garlic powder, oregano, and parsley.
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown. Slice into breadsticks and serve warm.
