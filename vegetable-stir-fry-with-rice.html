Vegetable Stir-Fry with Rice

Prep time: 15 mins

Yield: 4

Ingredients









Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add broccoli, carrots, bell peppers, and onion. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
  2. Add soy sauce, sesame oil, and grated ginger. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  3. Serve hot over cooked rice.
