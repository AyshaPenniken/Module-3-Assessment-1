Classic Vanilla Pudding

Prep time: 10 mins (+ 2 hours chilling)

Yield: 4

Ingredients





Steps

  1. In a saucepan, whisk together milk, sugar, cornstarch, and salt. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until thickened.
  2. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract.
  3. Pour into serving bowls, cover with plastic wrap, and chill for at least 2 hours before serving.
