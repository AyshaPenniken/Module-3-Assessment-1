Iced Mint Green Tea

Prep time: 10 mins

Yield: 4

Ingredients






Steps

  1. Boil water and steep green tea bags with mint leaves for 5 minutes.
  2. Remove tea bags and strain out mint leaves. Stir in honey if desired.
  3. Chill tea in the fridge, then serve over ice with lemon slices for garnish.
