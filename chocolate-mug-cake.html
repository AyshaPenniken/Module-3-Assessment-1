Chocolate Mug Cake

Prep time: 5 mins

Yield: 1

Ingredients








Steps

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Stir in milk, oil, and vanilla until smooth.
  3. Microwave on high for 1-1.5 minutes. Let cool slightly before enjoying.
