Apple Cinnamon Crumble

Prep time: 15 mins (+ 30 mins baking)

Yield: 6

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease a baking dish.
  2. Toss apples with sugar and cinnamon, then place in the dish.
  3. In a bowl, mix flour, oats, brown sugar, and melted butter until crumbly. Sprinkle over apples.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes or until topping is golden and apples are tender.
