Herbed Roasted Potatoes

Prep time: 10 mins

Yield: 4

Ingredients








Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, toss potato wedges with olive oil, garlic powder, rosemary, thyme, paprika, and salt.
  3. Spread potatoes in a single layer on the baking sheet. Roast for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden brown and crispy.
